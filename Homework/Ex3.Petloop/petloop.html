About pets

Pets are animals that people keep in their homes for companionship and entertainment. They have played an important role in human life for thousands of years. The most popular pets include dogs, cats, birds, fish, hamsters, and rabbits. Each of these animals has unique characteristics and care requirements. Scientific studies show that pets can reduce stress, improve mood, and even contribute to better physical health for their owners. Source: Adapted from Wikipedia and other educational resources
